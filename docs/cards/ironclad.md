# Ironclad (Red) Cards Reference

This document provides reference information for all Ironclad cards.

## Card ID Naming

Ironclad cards generally use **spaces in their IDs** (e.g., "Battle Trance", "Blood for Blood"). This is consistent with the game's display names for most cards.

## Key Synergies

### Strength Scaling
- `Inflame`, `Spot Weakness`, `Demon Form`, `Limit Break` - gain Strength
- `Heavy Blade` - 3x Strength multiplier
- `Sword Boomerang`, `Pummel`, `Whirlwind` - multi-hit benefits from Strength

### Exhaust Synergies
- `Feel No Pain` - block on Exhaust
- `Dark Embrace` - draw on Exhaust
- `Corruption` - Skills cost 0 but Exhaust
- `Fiend Fire`, `Second Wind`, `Sever Soul` - mass Exhaust

### Block Scaling
- `Body Slam` - damage equals block
- `Entrench` - double block
- `Barricade` - block persists
- `Juggernaut` - damage when blocking

### Self-Damage Synergies
- `Rupture` - Strength on HP loss from cards
- `Brutality`, `Combust`, `Offering`, `Bloodletting`, `Hemokinesis` - lose HP

### Strike Synergies
- `Perfected Strike` - +2 damage per Strike in deck
- Cards with STRIKE tag: Strike_R, Twin Strike, Wild Strike, Perfected Strike, Pommel Strike

## Notable Mechanics

### Infinitely Upgradeable
- `Searing Blow` is the only card that can be upgraded multiple times (+4 damage each)

### Self-Damage Cards
| Card | HP Cost | Benefit |
|------|---------|---------|
| Offering | 6 HP | 2 energy, draw 3 |
| Bloodletting | 3 HP | 2 energy |
| Hemokinesis | 2 HP | 15 damage |
| Brutality | 1 HP/turn | draw 1/turn |
| Combust | 1 HP/turn | 5 damage to ALL/turn |

### Multi-Hit Attacks
- `Sword Boomerang` - 3 random hits
- `Pummel` - 4 hits
- `Twin Strike` - 2 hits
- `Whirlwind` - X hits to ALL
