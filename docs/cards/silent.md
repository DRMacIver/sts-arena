# Silent (Green) Cards Reference

This document provides reference information for all Silent cards.

## Card ID Naming

Silent cards generally use **spaces in their IDs** (e.g., "A Thousand Cuts", "Cloak And Dagger"). Some notable IDs:

| Display Name | Card ID | Notes |
|--------------|---------|-------|
| Cloak and Dagger | `Cloak And Dagger` | Capital "And" |
| Dodge and Roll | `Dodge and Roll` | Lowercase "and" |
| Alchemize | `Alchemize` | HEALING tag |
| Underhanded Strike | `SneakyStrike` | Different ID format |

## Key Synergies

### Shiv Synergies
- `Accuracy` - Shivs deal +4 damage
- `Blade Dance`, `Cloak And Dagger`, `Infinite Blades`, `Storm of Steel` - generate Shivs
- `After Image` - block per card (including Shivs)
- `A Thousand Cuts` - damage per card
- `Finisher` - scales with attacks played

### Poison Synergies
- `Deadly Poison`, `Poisoned Stab`, `Bouncing Flask`, `Noxious Fumes` - apply Poison
- `Catalyst` - double Poison
- `Corpse Explosion` - explode on death
- `Bane` - double damage if Poisoned
- `Envenom` - apply Poison on unblocked damage

### Discard Synergies
- `Calculated Gamble`, `Acrobatics`, `Prepared`, `Survivor`, `Dagger Throw` - discard
- `Eviscerate` - costs less per discard
- `SneakyStrike` - gain energy if discarded
- `Concentrate` - discard for energy
- `Tingsha` (relic) - damage on discard
- `Tough Bandages` (relic) - block on discard

### Weak/Debuff Synergies
- `Heel Hook` - bonus on Weak enemies
- `Neutralize`, `Sucker Punch`, `Leg Sweep`, `Crippling Poison` - apply Weak
- `Terror` - 99 Vulnerable
- `Piercing Wail` - remove Strength

## Notable Mechanics

### Multi-Hit Attacks
| Card | Hits |
|------|------|
| Dagger Spray | 2 to ALL |
| Riddle with Holes | 5 |
| Glass Knife | 2 |
| Eviscerate | 3 |
| Skewer | X |

### 0-Cost Cards
- Neutralize, Slice, Backstab (Innate), Deflect, Prepared, Calculated Gamble
- Endless Agony, Masterful Stab (starts at 0), Concentrate, Escape Plan, Adrenaline
- Grand Finale (requires empty draw pile)

### Scaling Cards
- `Finisher` - damage per Attack played
- `Flechettes` - damage per Skill in hand
- `Eviscerate` - costs less per discard
- `Glass Knife` - damage decreases each play
- `Masterful Stab` - costs more per HP lost
